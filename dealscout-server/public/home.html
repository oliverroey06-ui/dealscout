Straight answers.

Which marketplaces does it scan?+
Genuine second-hand and resale marketplaces: eBay, Vinted, Gumtree and Shpock on every plan, plus premium connectors — Depop, StockX, Grailed, Vestiaire and Preloved — on Elite. All authentic goods; no counterfeit or replica sources.
How is the "going rate" worked out?+
From the live listings each search returns. DealScout throws out bundles, job-lots and mis-listings (so a £400 controller doesn't skew a £20 one), then takes the median of what's left. A listing far below that is flagged to verify, not celebrated.
What's the free trial?+
Verify a phone number at sign-up and get 7 days of Pro free — unlimited scans, bigger watchlist, advanced filters. One trial per phone number.
Can I cancel any time?+
Yes. Manage or cancel your subscription from your account page at any time, through Stripe's secure portal. No lock-in.
